The roster — 31 agents.

LeedAB ships up to 31 specialised agents. Each customer gets the subset their business actually needs — typically 5 to 15 of them, selected via the per-license-key manifest. A solo legal practice gets a different team than a 30-person logistics company. Below: every canonical agent, what they do, and which team they sit in.
